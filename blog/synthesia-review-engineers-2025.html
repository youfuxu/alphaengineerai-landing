What Is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that turns a script into a finished video featuring a realistic AI presenter — no camera, no studio, no editor. You type or paste text, pick an avatar and a voice, and it renders a talking-head video in minutes. It supports 140+ languages, a library of stock avatars, and the option to create a custom avatar of yourself.

It's built for business and content use cases: training videos, product explainers, marketing clips, course lessons, and faceless social content. For engineers, the appeal is the same as any good tool — it collapses a slow, manual production process into something repeatable, scriptable, and fast.
